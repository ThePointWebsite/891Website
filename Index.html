PET PICK OF THE WEEK!

Rainy

Rainy is very sweet and playful, but still quite shy. He needs special people who will give him the love and especially the time he needs to adjust to his new home. He will do best in a home with no small children or dogs. Rainy is a gray DLH who is about 3 months old, neutered and up to date on vaccines.
